baa airports car parking

Parking signage across all national airports had been operated by many third-party companies working side-by-side under different banners. The problem being no uniform strategy or management across airports, resulting in confused customers.

 

BAA worked with Ignite to streamline all signage for car parks nationally and create a much better customer experience.

+ hierarchy strategy

+ way-finding strategy
