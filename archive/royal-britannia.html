royal britannia full programme

The Royal Yacht Britannia has sailed over one million miles as roving ambassador for Great Britain. Now, at her new home in Edinburgh, you have the opportunity to entertain your guests within her State Apartments.

 

When Angela first contacted us she said "our corporate events are suffering. We need more sales & better sales!" A familiar problem that Ignite was tasked to solve.

 

It seemed a lot of potential clients weren't buying and many top potential corporate clients perceived TRYB as too expensive.

 

For Ignite, the priority was to experience the corporate event to fully understand the offer and then review all communications. After two visits
it was clear to us this was, and is one of the best hospitality experiences on offer in Scotland.

 

We worked very closely with the client to take the photography we felt reflected the experience and then produced a campaign (including a new high level brochure) to re-position The Royal Yacht
as the best corporate experience of its kind.

 

A 40% increase in sales was achieved and customers now see TRYB as great value for money despite an increase in costs.
