portavadie rebranding

Portavadie has been developing their marina and retreat over the last 5 years (on the stunning shores of Loch Fyne) to create something truly exceptional. The project was initially created to cater for the sailing community, but fast developed into something far more.

 

Portavadie now has a fantastic range of accomodation, from luxury apartments, cosy cottages & studio apartments to an eco-retreat on the shore. This is supported with eating facilities, marina, a small shop & spa treatments. This escape is perfect for family breaks, romantic weekend retreats for two or an active holidays with friends. And is in the process of expanding to include an indoor swimming pool as well as a full service luxury spa

 

We were initially brought in to look at a range of projects, and through a process, we encouraged the client to go back to basics (including leading a brand workshop) to uncover the essence of what was special at Portavadie. The result is, better tailored marketing, underpinned by a well thought out strategic approach, and brand image - which now fits Portavadie perfectly.
