Science is all lies unless it’s based on empirical evidence. Upon observation
and interrogation. At Ignite Your Brand, we offer in-depth strategy programmes
and brand workshops. We analyse you and your competitors. And we speak to your customers. Discovering, understanding, communicating. Finding the point of difference
and creating a strategy to maximise the opportunities it presents.

The Science
Who are you? Where are you going? We begin with a definition. Yours.
Through our Brand Workshop Programme we'll discover your strengths and your weaknesses. What makes you unique, or, at least, different. Then we bring it all together
to define or refine your brand values, who your customers are, what makes them tick
and more importantly what they truly want.

 

Who else is out there? It’s difficult to know how good, or bad, you are without a frame of reference. By undertaking a Competitor Analysis Programme, you’ll understand in much more depth, your place in the grand scheme of things and how you stack up against the competition.

 

And who are the most important people? They’re your worst critics and your biggest fans. But how well do you really know your customers? In a Customer Survey Programme,
we’ll help you find out and turn their dreams and desires into meaningful realities.

 

Now, you may know all this already...

So you're sure of who you are, who your competitors are, and what your customers want. Time to implement some creativity. The Art.
