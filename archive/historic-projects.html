adobe systems various
Over 14 years we completed an extensive range
of projects for Adobe Europe, including: global exhibitions, bundle packaging, product brochures, sales promotion, sales brochures and digital campaigns. We worked with a number of brand managers across the whole organisation. The company consolidated, closing the European office.

visit scotland overseas guides
Working with the overseas team, we produced overseas guides in 8 languages and printed over a million copies each year. Designing and artworking the whole guide from start to finish including section layout, typesetting, designing adverts and working with the full time photographer. We worked on this project for 7 years.

scottish ballet various
Each performance was unique, each audience individual. The one thing in common was they all presented the best in ballet. From the much loved ‘Nutcracker’ to the more obscure ‘Tales of Hoffmann’ each one was a fantasia of image and dance. We captured this on each occasion and achieved a huge customer response.

edinburgh airport partners brochures
Our task was to create a meaningful brochure to highlight the benefits of choosing Edinburgh Airport as a destination worth connecting prospective airlines to. The concept focusses on the benefits
of Edinburgh as the key selling point - the main reason most passengers would choose this airport is more to do with the famous capital city.

increation interiors brochure
Bespoke, Crafted, Interiors. There are few occasions an interior designer will take the client on a journey of discovery to find out what they like in colour, mood and experience (and then build from scratch in a cabinet making workshop completely bespoke). Our skill was to capture this unique process and deliver it with style.

capital solutions rebrand
From brand strategy to some very creative thinking, emerged a new brand. A brand which expressed the value existing in the world of multifunctional devices from an organisation passionate about service and delivery. This new brand was launched overnight to a very receptive audience. Sophisticated and playful.

cameron ross brochure
We created one of the most outstanding brochures this organisation had ever seen. No surprise to find this was the challenge set by the client.
The photography was shot in Blienham Palace over
5 days with a cast of many. We managed the whole project from start to finish. The brochure became
a new standard in this industry.

french duncan brand building
With the aim to compete with the big four in the UK, French Duncan used our help to realise their brand vision. With only a logo as the main brand identity, the first task was to create and implement a unique brand personality, ‘More than just numbers’. The point of difference is French Duncan are a whole lot more than just number crunching.

von essen hotels wedding brochure
We were commissioned by the Von Essen Group to design a bespoke, luxury wedding brochure which could be used for each hotel within the group.
It was important to communicate the very high level experience provided by the group, from helicopter and Bentley transport to hand picked fresh flowers on the day.

rsno astar children’s cd
With government funding in place to present a CD
to every child born in Scotland over the next 12 months, RSNO turned to Ignite to help package it. We created beautiful illustrations that ran throughout  a colourful sleeve and booklet, informing parents on how best to use the music during their child's day.

centotre branding & interior
The brief: “We want to be a restaurant that provides great value, but not cheap. And stylish
but not pretentious like Harvey Nichols – you were recommended to me, how can you help?”.
We totally branded this business, from strategy, concept positioning, competitor analysis though to interior design, graphic design and implementation.

roml online thrill seeking
The client set out to create a portal concept which
allowed thrill seekers (or ‘Weekend Warriors’) to
pick the level of weekend adventure escape they
required online, anywhere in Scotland.
This was achieved through the creation of clever
technology combined with social networking with
Experts and Enthusiasts.

acanthus architects df brochure
AADF champions new design and conservation. Since their inception in 1981, they have built up an extensive library of work. It was up to us to understand them thoroughly and then collate 28 years worth of work and condense it all into an album that would detail the essence of who they are and what they do brilliantly.

caledonia homes projects
Originally our job with Caledonia Homes was to create a series of identities and collateral for their 1930′s style developments built around Scotland. This is an example of one of their premier developments, which needed something a little bit more sophisticated.

miller group property branding
Merchant Exchange is just one of the many projects we completed for Miller Group over the years.
This project was created to sell an old building which had been converted into contemporary flats. We created a look and feel which reflected this
and was developed into many bits of collateral, from brochures to adverts.

domino's pizza tactical marketing
Dominoid; a franchise group of Domino's, requires tactical marketing to top up sales at certain times of the year. So far,  we have worked on a full range of campaigns,  from miniature pizza boxes to local van liveries for charities and clubs as well as sub identities for University pizza societies.
