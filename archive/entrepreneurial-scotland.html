entrepreneurial scotland rebranding

The existing organisations; ‘Entrepreneurial Exchange’ and the 'Saltire Foundation' had been going for some years, with their own individual strengths. The Saltire was global connected with a phenomenal alumni network and the Exchange had some of the most influential business leaders collaborating within its network. By combining the talents of young, and not so young nascent entrepreneurs with seasoned entrepreneurs from the Exchange there was room to build a substantive force for change.

 

Our task was first to consider a name change for the exchange and then to reflect these new inspirational organisations for what they are when combined - a joining of all of the dots, a movement, a community who passionately believes, together they can revolutionise the dynamic growth of the Scottish economy.

 

Working very closely with the main team at ES to communicate the growing change of Scotland's entrepreneurial community. 'Entrepreneurial Scotland', now, more than ever, along with 'The Saltire Foundation', commits to find, inspire, develop and connect current and future high impact entrepreneurial leaders worldwide, to ignite economic growth in Scotland.
