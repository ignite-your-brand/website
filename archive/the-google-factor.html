So, unless you’ve been under a rock these past few weeks you’ll have seen the much anticipated Google rebrand that took place a couple of weeks ago. Now, I realise that this may not mean much to you or even change the world you live in, but for us branding geeks, this was quite the event. The departure from the “old” look and feel to the new is not huge, in fact it was so subtle that most people didn’t even notice.

So why you ask?

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

 

There was some legal stuff relating to the Security and Exchange Commission because Google had created a new public holding company: Alphabet Inc. But why change the whole Brand and more to the point, is it worth it?

The answer: YES! But don’t just take my word for it. By 5pm on the day the new “look & feel” Google was announced the share price was up nearly 5.5%. And we’re still talking about it nearly 3 months down the line.

We, at Ignite Your Brand, are ready to chat to you and your team about how you can emulate the Google Factor.
