wood group new brand identity
This multi-faceted new brand identity reflects the

multi-dimentional, multi-capable, multi-skilled,
and collectively talented teams who work within
the many divisions of Wood Group. With over 45,000 employees (and a turnover of 7bn), it is
a symbol which had to be enormously flexible for its diverse, global stakeholders and customers.

entrepreneurial scotland rebranding
Our first task was to consider a name change and new brand logo for this new inspirational, high impact organisation. We are currently working
on a new website to help communicate more of
the power of collaboration, connection, support
and vision for this dynamic community.

trump international coat of arms
With a proven track record of working in the high

end leisure market, including Gleneagles Hotel

and The Royal Yacht Britannia, we were

commissioned by Trump International Golf Links,

Scotland, to assist with the development

of an international marque following the

authorisation of a Scottish corporate coat of arms.

landbase new brand identity
We have been working with Landbase for over a year now, initially on strategy and brand identity, and latterly on unique retail space design. We created this quirky bit of magic to reflect the people and its culture at the core of the company, as well as the wholesome food imported from around the world (to be distributed throughout China).

baa car parking
Parking signage across all national airports had

become out of control, from a coherent design

point of view, over the years. The result was very

confused customers with less than efficient

journeys. BAA worked with Ignite to streamline
all signage for these car parks to create a much better customer experience.

gleneagles hotel marketing
We have been working with Gleneagles for over
15 years on an expansive variety of projects, from

Stategic to tactical, large and small. From bespoke

menus to direct mail, wedding brochures, sales

presenters and seasonal ownership marketing.
In each case the core message is always the same,

Gleneagles is one of the best resorts in the world.

The housing marketed is starting to boom again,

programmes to help Robertson Homes do just that.

kitchens international branding
Kitchens International are Scotland's leading high

quality kitchen designers who mix function with

style at the highest level to give you most fabulous

cooking and entertaining space possible. We have

been working with KI for over 7 years on a full

re-brand programme which has continued to bear

fruit year on year, from positioning to bottow line.

gs group rebrand
George Stubbs Insurance are Scotlands largest

privately owned insurance brokers who have grown

dramatically through being one of the most trusted

and pro-active companies in their sector. And they

had grown up significantly. Our work involved

working very closely with them to re-positioning

the company to reflect this new larger brand.

simon howie brand development
Working very closely with Simon Howie, we

enhanced the brand by creating a visual story

(through images and film) of him, the farmers son,

growing up to become a excellent independent

butcher, supplying both direct to consumer and to

trade. This story has been translated onto the

packaging, a brand book, and commercials.

mackays repositioning brand
Mackays was founded in 1938 by the Mackay

Brothers. Today, owned by the Grant family and

based in Scotland, they are proud of their heritage

and authentic products. We worked closely with

them to elevate the brand to a position of higher

value to reflect the quality of their traditionally

made jams, marmalades and preserves.

portavadie repositioning brand
Set on the Cowall Peninsula, where Loch Fyne
flows to firth and sea, Portavadie is a beautiful

destination for family holidays, sailing adventures

and inspired events. Our ongoing task is to reflect

this in the communication. We have been working

on the brand re-positioning for over a year and

starting to see very positive results.

grant property repositioning brand
Grant property is a marketing, letting and

management property company who helps

international investors to build portfolios

throughout the UK. A major opportunity for Grant

Property is to engage with larger investment funds

to help net larger projects. The re-brand is

underway to reflect this new way of thinking.

exotissimo brand development
Exotissimo is one of Asia’s largest destination

management travel companies who create and

deliver unique, bespoke packages for international

clients who want to provide the most authentic

escapes for their customers. We are undertaking

strategy programmes to help develop the brand

communications to reflect this.

skinnifit graphic communications

with the SF (Skinnifit) brand to promote its

products to to a wide market of resellers,

strathbraan brewery new brand
Strathbraan is a family owned small Perthshire

producing new labels and bottle design to match

the companies growth and breadth in the market.

stonehouses new brand
When Gavin started developing two unique holiday homes, he understood that getting the right brand image was as important as the architecture and the interior. lubiju new brand
