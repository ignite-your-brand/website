grant property rebranding

Grant Property is a leading letting agent and investment specialist who helps high net worth individuals invest in safe property products and diverse portfolios within the residential property market. It is one of the few organisations within Scotland who help purchase, let and manage property to provide clients with the full investment package for clients living abroad.

 

The company had, over the years, grown its reputation within the student market and in recent times moved into more high end property development.

 

We were tasked with creating a new brand identity to reflect the changing organisation and market needs. Subsequently, and due to our unique strategy process -which uncovered a breadth of expertise and valuable propositions - we developed a comprehensive brand identity with life, range and adaptability.
