Why contact George?

There are times you just need a bit of reflection, you need the other point of view.

Brands, like people, change and grow over time. Your products and services change

to keep up with customer demand, and markets change all too quickly.

 

My experience tells me it is far too easy to get myopic and sometimes very difficult

to see the wood for the trees. I am passionate about helping organisations define

and promote their products and services within Scotland and the UK.

To help them thrive, and to be their best, succeeding beyond all expectations.

 

 

Some of the reasons I get a call:

 

+ Market competition has grown

+ Visual brand is dissipated through inconsistent use

+ Sales channels have changed

+ Brand is not being recognised by the target audience

+ Products and services offered changes

+ Markets needs and requirements change

+ Target audiences has changed

+ Corporate structure has changed or key staff have left

+ Brand is tired and/or has been overtaken in the marketplace

+ Profit is hard to net, it was easier in the past

+ Opportunity to expand the brand

 

If you would like a little bit of outside perspective on your brand, over an informal

cuppa, contact me on my direct number or e-mail. I would be delighted to help.
