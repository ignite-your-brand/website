zanzero full programme

After the highly successful launch of Centotre (branded by Ignite), Victor and Carina Contini commissioned us to create their a new brand
for their next, cafe-bar.

 

The brief we created, post strategy programmes, articulated the need for a contemporary, vibrant, cafe bar in a much smaller scale than Centotre
to help scale the business. This new concept should be franchisable, quirky, easy to duplicate and follow some of the values of Centotre.

 

A bright zingy identity and contemporary interior design was developed and created to reflect all
of the needs discovered in all the brand strategy work done to date. The result was an unusual version of an italian cafe-bar, something with
a little bit of zing.
