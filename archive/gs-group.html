gs group rebranding

GS Group is amongst the largest independent insurance brokers in the UK. Using a pro-active approach with clients, they deliver bespoke insurance and financial solutions to the business community in Scotland and beyond. Key to this
is a friendly, detail obsessed and professional approach to bring clients risk protection and peace of mind.

 

GS Group had grown quickly in recent times, developing services and market share extensively and needed to reflect this new stronger, more comprehensive company in thier brand image.

 

Our main task was to re-position GS Insurance
to reflect a larger and more experienced group
of experts. We took GS Insurance through an inclusive brand strategy programme to help them articulate who they were at the core, what made them different and why their customers loved them. And then created new communications -
for the internal audience as well as external -
to reflect this new more progressive Group.
