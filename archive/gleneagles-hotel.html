gleneagles branding

The setting is beautiful. The facilities impeccable.
The service; well, the service is simply world class. There’s only one thing missing at Gleneagles. Your company. To help the sales team at Gleneagles book corporate events we devised a little leave behind to remind potential clients of this unsurpassed venue. A digital photo frame uploaded with emotional imagery to show that Gleneagles has the best people, delivering the best service in the best resort in the world. Best come and see for yourself.

 

Glenmor Seasonal Ownership is a collection of exclusive cottages set within the spectacular grounds of the Gleneagles Resort. The cottages are truly a home from home, built in the traditional Scottish Vernacular and with five star luxury interiors to match. And after a short introduction to Glenmor and a tour of the Gleneagles Resort potential customers are given a mini spa treatment as a gift and a wee reminder. What better way to soak up the atmosphere.

 

We have been working with Gleneagles for over 14 years in a huge range of projects, from Stategic to tactical, large and small. Only a fraction of these projects are featured here.
