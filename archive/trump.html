trump coat of arms

The central focus of the Trump development
is the golf course designed by Dr. Martin Hawtree,
the acknowledged master of links golf course
architecture. The championship golf course
is a classical Scottish links. The course has
a Par of 72 and a maximum length of just
over 7400 yards. The layout of the world class
championship links course complements the natural
landscape and offer golfers dramatic sea views from
nearly every hole. The course is both challenging
and beautiful for golfers of all levels.

 

With a proven track record of working in the high
end leisure market, including Gleneagles Hotel
and The Royal Yacht Britannia, we were commissioned by Trump International Golf Links, Scotland, to assist with the development
of an international marque following the authorisation of a Scottish corporate coat of arms. This brand identity went on everything from embroidery to stationery in time for the launch
of this world class golf course.
