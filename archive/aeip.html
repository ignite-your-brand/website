When Buccleuch Property bought over two long standing science parks in Aberdeen they knew that re-branding them was a vital part of their renewall.

 

After undertaking a brand programme to help
the client clarify their target audiences, brand values and sales messages, Ignite Your Brand created a bright, new flexible brand identity
to cover both parks.

 

To unify the experience of both tenants and visitors, we worked with the client's architect on interior design improvements for communal areas and external totem and wayfinding signage.

 

The client also required a website that not only allowed  the current tenants to communicate with each other, but also sold the park to interested parties. This was backed up by a full range
of printed sales literature and advertising.
