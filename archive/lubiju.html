lubiju branding & interior design

Lubiju provides a luxurious, first class experience

in advanced cosmetic and restorative procedures, to discerning clients seeking to improve their self-confidence and well being.

 

To help launch this leading dento-facial practice, Ignite were commissioned to undertake a full brand strategy programme (including a brand workshop and competitor analysis) followed by
a naming project as well as a new identity, website and full interior design.

 

An elegant simple namestyle was developed and

balanced with bright, fresh lifestyle images and zesty graphics to reflect this new kind of dental practice. This brand language was also carried into the interior design of the entrance and waiting areas.
